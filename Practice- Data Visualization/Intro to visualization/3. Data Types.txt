Qualitative or categorical types (카테고리 및 범주형)
1. Nominal data:
-pure labels without inherent order
-(no label is intrinsically greater or less than any other)
-국가, 음식

2. Ordinal data:
-labels with an intrinsic order or ranking
-(comparison operations can be made between values, but the magnitude of differences are not be well-defined)
-랭크(1등, 2등 3등)
-설문조사 (좋음 평균 나쁨)

Quantitative or numeric types (숫자형)
3. Interval data:
-numeric values where absolute differences are meaningful
-(addition and subtraction operations can be made)
-온도
-설문조사 (좋음 평균 나쁨은 1,2,3으로도 계산될수 있으므로 범주 및 숫자 둘다 가능!)

4. Ratio data:
-numeric values where relative differences are meaningful
-(multiplication and division operations can be made)
-무게, 키
